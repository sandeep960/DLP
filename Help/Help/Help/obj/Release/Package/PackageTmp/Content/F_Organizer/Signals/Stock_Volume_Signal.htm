Stock Volume Signal

This signal filters symbols that match volume requirements for current volume, directional trending volume or directional consecutive volume.

 

Examples:

Current:

If you want to select symbols with the previous trading day's volume 2 times greater than the 30 day avg volume and over 500,000 shares: select Interest=Current, Operator=>=, Times=2, Avg Days=30 and Interest=500,000.

Trend:

If you want to select symbols that have upward trending volume for 5 consecutive days: select Interest=Trend, Direction=Increasing and Avg Days=5.

Consecutive:

If you want to find stocks that have had consecutive volume declines over the past 5 days: select Interest=Consecutive, Direction=Decreasing and Days=5.
