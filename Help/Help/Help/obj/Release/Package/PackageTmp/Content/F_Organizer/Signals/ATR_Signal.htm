ATR Signal

Average True Range (ATR) is a stock's range between the high and low price on any given day. It reveals information about how volatile a stock is. Large ranges indicate high volatility and small ranges indicate low volatility. The range is measured the same way for options (high minus low) as they are for stocks.

 

Example:

To find symbols that have a 14 day ATR value between 1 and 2: select Days=14, Min=1 and Max=2.
