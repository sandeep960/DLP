Option Spread Signal

Liquidity is an important factor in options trading. There are several ways one can identify the liquid symbols. In general, the more the demand, the more the symbols are liquid.

Option Spread finds average (ask-bid) liquidity from all symbols. Then this average/close price as percentage should be between 0% and 1% in the selection on the right side.

 

Example:

To select symbols that have tight bid ask spread within the range of 0.10% to 1.00% of its own close price on the header date: select Min=0.10 and Max=1.00.
