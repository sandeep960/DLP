Weekly Trade Wizard

Weekly Trades Backtest is designed to help you design a strategy based on systematic weekly calendar trading.

Backtest using weekly calendar trading strategies such as Iron Condors, and variations of butterflies as a way to generate income.

 

In this section, we are going to show you how to perform a BackTest in the Weekly Trade Modeling Wizard by using an example:

  • We are going to BackTest a Bullish strategy for QQQ. 
  • We are going to sell one weekly put on QQQ for the past year.
  • The naked put will have the following characteristics:
  • Enter the trade on the previous Thursday and exit on the Friday expiration
  • Days to expiration: minimum of 3 days to maximum of 12 days
  • Strike Selection: 30-45 Delta Range
  • Profit target: 100% of Maximum Profit
  • Stop loss: 50% of Initial Risk

 

There are 3 steps to complete this BackTest: Entry-Exit Dates >> Trade Rules >> Preview

Entry-Exit Dates:

  1. Input a name for the dates to be saved under QQQ Naked Put. 
  2. Input the stock symbol as QQQ. 
  3. Input the date range to be tested in From Date 05/05/2016 and To Date 05/05/2017.
  4. Select the day of the week you want to enter the trade as Pwd-Thursday.
  5. Select the day of the week you want to exit the trade as Friday and Skip 1 Weekly expiration.
  6. Click to save your Entry-Exit Dates and to move on to Trade Rules.

Trade Rules:

  1. Input a name for the trade rules to be saved under QQQ Naked Put. 
  2. Click the strategy drop down menu and select Naked Put.
  1. Input the minimum and maximum days to expiration as 3 and 12.
  2. Input the minimum and maximum delta range as 30 and 45.
  3. Input the profit target % as 100 based off the MaxProfit.
  4. Input the stop loss % as 50 based off the CurrentRisk.
  5. Click to save your Trade Rules and to move on to Preview.

Preview:

All fields will auto-populate.  For risk per trade, you can select a dollar amount/trade, contracts/trade or a custom amount created manually.

  1. Click to have the system run the tests.

  2. Select to go to the BackTest results page.
