IV Ranker Wizard

IV Rank Backtest is designed to help you design a strategy using Implied Volatility. Implied volatility can make an option cheap/expensive on a relative basis. Backtest options strategy based on rise or decline in Implied Volatility Rank.

 

In this section, we are going to show you how to perform a BackTest in the IV Ranker Modeling Wizard by using an example:

  • We are going to BackTest a Bullish strategy for IWM. 
  • We are going to open one bull call spread on IWM when the 30 day IV Ranker increases into Q2 and exit the trade when the IV Ranker increases into Q3.
  • The bull call spread will have the following characteristics:
  • Days to expiration: minimum of 30 days to maximum of 60 days
  • Strike Selection:

    • Short Call: 35-50 Delta range

    • Minimum width between calls: $1
    • Long Call: 40-55 Delta range

  • Profit target: 25% of the Initial Cost

  • Stop loss: 100% of the Initial Cost

 

There are 3 steps to complete this BackTest: Entry-Exit Dates >> Trade Rules >> Preview

Entry-Exit Dates:

  1. Input a name for the dates to be saved under IWM IV Ranker. 
  2. Select the stock you want to test IWM. 
  3. Input the date range to be tested in From Date 01/03/2007 and To Date 05/08/2017.
  4. Select the IV Days as 30.
  5. Select the Entry Direction as Increase and the Entry Quartile as Q2.
  6. Select the Exit Direction asIncrease and the Exit Quartile as Q3.
  7. Click to save your Entry-Exit Dates and to move on to Trade Rules.

Trade Rules:

  1. Input a name for the trade rules to be saved under IWM Bull Call Spread. 
  2. Click the Strategy drop down menu and select Bull Call with Market as Spread.
  3. Input the minimum and maximum days to expiration as 30 and 60.
  4. Input the minimum and maximum delta range as:
  • Long Call (Buy1) 40 and 55
  • Short Call (Sell1) 35 and 50
  1. Input the minimum width between the calls as 1.
  2. Enter the profit target % as 25 based off the InitialCost.
  3. Enter the stop loss % as 100 based off the InitialCost.
  4. Click to save your Trade Rules and to move on to Preview.
