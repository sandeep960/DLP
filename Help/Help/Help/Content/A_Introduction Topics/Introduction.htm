INTRODUCTION

Delphian is an institutional strength market analysis, strategy development, optimization, and execution platform for quant managers, investment banks, academia, money managers, proprietary traders and retail traders.

 

The Delphian platform has two main functions:

  • State Modeling, our proprietary Buy/sell signal and
  • The ability for our users to create and backtest their trading strategies to find optimal trades.

State Modeling

Our proprietary State Modeling™ uses a machine learning algorithm identifies patterns in the market and gives traders everything they need to know to enter high probability trades and maximize profits. State Modeling is based on discrete mathematics called “finite-state machine” or finite state automata. What this means is that at any given time, a stock is classified into one state based on a set of criteria. When the criteria changes it moves into another state. It is much like a traffic light moving from Green to Yellow to Red.

BackTest

Delphian’s institutional grade backtesting makes it easy for people with no programming experience to test various theories and strategies using historical data to determine “What if I had applied investment strategy X during period Y.” This level of quantitative modeling is typically is only available to large institutions or people with vast computer programming experience. Delphian makes it easy for traders to optimize and improve their strategies, find any technical or theoretical flaws, and gain confidence in their strategy before applying it to the real markets.
