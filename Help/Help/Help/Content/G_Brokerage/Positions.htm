Positions

The Positions page displays the open, pending, closed and link positions of the trade.

Open Positions

This displays the current active positions that have been filled.

After an order is created, the order is converted to the open position. Tradier provides it an ORDER ID and POSITION ID.

 

You can click on the symbol to view its open, high, low, close and last price.

Closing an open position:

User can close leg or the entire position by clicking on the in the leg close column.

Pending Order Positions

This displays the active orders that are unfilled.

If leg position or entire position is closed in Delphian Trading, this leg or position is updated as pending, and considered as pending order positions.

These orders exist in Delphian Trading until filled in Tradier.

Closed Positions

This displays the history of all previously executed positions.

Link Positions

This links the open positions with the same symbol created in Tradier brokerage to form a position.

Distinct symbols are displayed in the dropdown and after selecting the particular symbol, open positions [STO, BTO Legs] are displayed with check boxes. To link option legs one needs to check and click Link. This reloads the Open Positions tab with with linking positions.

De-Link Positions

Linked open positions can be de-linked to individual legs any time before they expire or manually closed by user from De-Link. De-Link separates the position to individual legs and open positions are reloaded.
