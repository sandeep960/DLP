Compare

The Compare option lets the user to compare two or more stock symbols.

Input two or more stock symbols in the symbol field to see the result.

The field is auto populated by entering the first few alphabets of the symbol and selecting from the list.

 

The comparison can be done based on a time range of 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, Year to Date (YTD), 1 year or All. The All time range starts from January 3, 2007 to the previous trading day date.
