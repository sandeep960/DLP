Price % Change Signal

This Signal filters for symbols that have certain % change over a certain period of days and in the direction you are looking for.

 

Example:

To find stocks with price increase percentage between 5% to 150% over the last 20 days, select 20 in days column, 5% in Min column and input 150 in Max column.

If you do not see preset % change, you can type in the desired % change.
