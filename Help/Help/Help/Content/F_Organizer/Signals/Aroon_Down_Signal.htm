Aroon Down Signal

Aroon Down is a measure of how close the current day’s closing price is from the most recent lowest closing price found in the last x amount of days.

 

Example:

Current

To find symbols that have Aroon Down value less than 10 over the past 25 trading days: select Aroon Down=Current, Days=25, Operator= “<” and Value=10.

 

Trend

To select symbols that have an downward trending 25 day Aroon Down for 4 consecutive days: select Aroon Down=Trend, Days=25, Direction=Decreasing and For Days=4.

 

Consecutive

If you want to select symbols that have an increasing 25 day Aroon Down for 7 consecutive days: select Aroon Down=Consecutive, Days=25, Direction=Increasing and For Days=7.
