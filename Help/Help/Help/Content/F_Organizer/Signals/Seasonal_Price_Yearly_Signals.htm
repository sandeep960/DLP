Seasonal Price Yearly Signals

The seasonal price yearly signal generates a list of symbols based on user defined criteria for upcoming time period, required return and number of occurrences over the last 10 years.

 

Example:

To find symbols that have had a price change greater than +10% in the coming 20 trading days, 7 out of the last 10 years: select Days=20, Operator=>, Positive=+10%, Negative=N/A and Num=7.
