State Targets Signal

This signal is created based on State Modelling™ technique. It allows users to find symbols based on their current state and whether or not their targets or stops losses have be met.

 

Example:

To find symbols that are in State 7 and have met their second target: select States=State 7, Operator=Met and Targets=Target 2.
