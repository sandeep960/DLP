BB Signal

Bollinger Bands are volatility bands placed above and below a moving average. Volatility is based on the standard deviation, which changes as volatility increases and decreases. The bands automatically widen when volatility increases and narrow when volatility decreases.

 

Example:

OHLC (Open, High, Low, Close)

To find symbols that closed greater than the upper bound of the 2 standard deviation 20 day exponential moving average: select Fields=Close, Operator=Greater Than Equal, Field2=Upper Bound, SD=2 and Days=20.

 

BB Bands

To find symbols that have squeezing bands of the 1.5 standard deviation 50 day exponential moving average: select Fields=BB Bands, Operator=Squeezing, SD=1.5 and Days=50.
