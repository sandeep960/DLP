+DI Signal

This signal is a component of the ADX that is used to measure the presence of an uptrend. When the +DI is sloping upward, it signals that the uptrend is getting stronger.

 

Example:

Current:

If you want to find symbols with a 14 day +DI value greater than or equal to -DI: select +DI=Current, Days=14, Operator=”>=” and Value= -DI.

 

Trend:

If you want to find symbols with their 14 day +DI value in an uptrend for the past 5 days: select +DI=Trend, Days=14, Direction=Increasing and For Days=5.

 

Consecutive:

If you want to find symbols with their 28 day +DI value increasing every day for the past 7 days: select +DI=Consecutive, Days=28, Direction=Increasing and Days=7.
